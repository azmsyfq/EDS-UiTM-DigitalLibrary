CHILDREN'S STORYBOOKS ONLINE

Publishing Links

While Children's Storybooks Online does not solicit stories, if you're interested in publishing your children's book/story on the web or in a more traditional manner, the following links may be of help.

SITES INTERESTED IN STORY SUBMISSIONS
(MOST NEITHER PAY FOR NOR REQUIRE A FEE FOR YOUR STORY)

Accepts Children's Stories Written By Adults

BAB Books On-Line Stories For Kids - A nice selection of stories written and illustrated by different people. Also has an "Adlib" area where you can create your own funny story.  (Sep. 1998)
Bedtime-Story - Whimsical bedtime stories for children of all ages.

Accepts Children's Stories/Poetry Written By Children

TheCase.com for Kids - Mysteries to solve, scary stories, magic tricks and contests  (Jul. 2000)
Cyberkids - Accepts stories, poems and art from children ages 7 to 12.  (Jul. 2000)
Cyke, Cyber Knowledge and Emotion - This site has fun interactive games for young children as well as animated stories. They accept story submittals from adults and children at: Submissions  (Jul. 2001)
Drew's Animals - A Christian children's site with Bible verses, fun games, coloring pages, online paper doll, and other games. The site has lots of animal photos as well as sketches by children. Children can submit their drawings to be put online.  (Jun. 2001)
gigglepoetry.com - Accepts original poetry from children.  (Jul. 2000)
Stories from the Web - This site encourages children's participation with their own stories, reviews and Web club. Cute site with lots to do.  (Jun. 2001)
Young Writers' Clubhouse - Advice, inspiration and contests for kids 10 and older. Features live chat, bulletin board and stories about young writers  (Mar. 2003)

Accepts Children's Stories Written By Adults Or Children

Storitime Publishing - Accepts and publishes stories by both children and adults. Writers and illustrators wanted. Submissions page at: submissions

OFFERING SELF-PUBLISHING FROM THE WEB

Emily and Tess's Story Page - Children are welcome to participate in this ongoing story.  (Jul. 2000)
Xlibris - Publishes and sells bound books off the web for as little as $500 or with Premium Service for $1600 and you keep your copyright.

GENERAL PUBLISHING INFORMATION

Bloomsburymagazine.com - An on-line magazine dedicated to information about writing. Also has reading guides and reading groups.  (Jun. 2001)
Children's Books Central - Has internet resources related to books for children and young adults. A meta site for the writers, readers, collectors, illustrators, librarians, teachers, parents, publishers, printers, storytellers and kids.  (Nov. 1998)
Children's Writing Resource Center - Lots of good information.

MISCELLANEOUS SERVICES

Creativemoonlighter.com - The ultimate resource for top freelance talent who will bid on your work. Find computer experts, illustrators, etc.  (Mar. 2003)
Guaranteed Proofreading Services - Reliable and reasonable.  (Mar. 2003)
