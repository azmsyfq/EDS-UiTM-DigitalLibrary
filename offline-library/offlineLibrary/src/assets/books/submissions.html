Story Submissions

Children's Storybooks Online is a hobby site and only a small amount of money is generated from banners. Therefore, we are unable to pay authors for their stories although they do retain all rights. In addition, any stories added to our site must have illustrations and, if not already provided, I must draw them or pay for artwork, which is difficult due to very limited time and revenue. With these constraints we can add only a few stories each year.

Please take the above into account when submitting a story. Unfortunately, there's been many lovely stories we weren't able to publish.

Carol Moore
