  Two Sided Online Jigsaw Puzzles
More difficult than our standard puzzles for those who like a challenge.
Double sided puzzles have different images front and back, and start with some pieces facing up and some down. You must choose which to flip over. These puzzles also come with and without piece rotation enabled. Puzzles with rotation enabled are designated with an "r", such as "56r".
